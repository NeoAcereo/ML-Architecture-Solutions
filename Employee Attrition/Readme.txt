Employee Attrition Classification Task

Classifying whether an employee is likely to come into attrition based information of the employee.

Dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shrutipandit707/hremployeeattritiondataset

Models: Trained cleaned and altered dataset on SVM, Logistic Regression, and Decision Tree classifiers, then moved on to ensembles using these
three.

Evaluation: Evaluated the models based on their accuracy, precision, and recall, and selected the best performing model for each metric.
